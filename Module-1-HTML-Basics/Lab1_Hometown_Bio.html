Kansas City,MO

Location

Kansas City is located in the heart of the Midwest.

About

City of Fountains & BBQ:

With over 200 fountains, it's called the "City of Fountains," and it also has more barbecue restaurants per capita than any other U.S. city.

Pioneer Trailhead & Jazz History:

The area was the starting point for major trails like the Oregon and Santa Fe Trails, and its history includes a vibrant, “Prohibition-era” jazz scene that earned it the nickname "Paris of the Plains".

Home to Major Brands:

Kansas City is where Hallmark Cards founder Joyce Hall started selling postcards, Russell Stover began making chocolates, and Walt Disney opened his first animation studio, Laugh-O-Gram.

Things To Do

  • Immerse Yourself in Jazz History - Explore the 18th & Vine Historic Jazz District, home to the American Jazz Museum, featuring interactive exhibits on jazz greats, listening stations, and live music.
  • Visit the National Museum of Toys & Miniatures - Explore the world's largest collection of fine-scale miniatures and antique toys at this UMKC-affiliated museum.
  • Explore Union Station & Its Surroundings - Visit the historic Union Station, a stunning architectural landmark, and discover Science City (interactive science center) and the National WWI Museum and Memorial nearby.
