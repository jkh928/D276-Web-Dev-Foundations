Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande-Butera (born June 26, 1993) is an American singer and actress who rose from Nickelodeon's Victorious and Sam & Cat to become a global pop superstar known for her powerful, Mariah Carey-esque vocals, chart-topping albums like Thank U, Next, and hits like "7 Rings" and "Positions," earning numerous awards and becoming a significant cultural figure with a distinct R&B-infused pop style.
